I am currently a second-year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Penn State, advised by Dr. Gang Tan. I also obtained my M.S. in Computer Science at Penn State. Before that, I did my Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics at Sun Yat-sen University.
